💸 APP MONETIZATION STRATEGY (FREEMIUM MODEL)
Here’s how we’ll split free vs. paid features — and I’ll give you options to pick from:

🆓 FREE FEATURES (HOOK THEM IN)
Feature	Why It’s Free
AI Chatbot (Basic Access)	Users can ask 3–5 questions/day or limited fitness/nutrition advice.
Beginner Bodyweight Workouts	Get people started with no equipment. Makes the app feel instantly valuable.
Daily Fitness Tip or Quote	From you or the AI trainer — builds habit + trust.
One Weekly Challenge	“Try this weekly bodyweight challenge.” Gives a taste of the premium version.
Shop Access	Let them browse your Printify merch anytime (this builds your brand).
Progress Log (Limited)	Basic tracking – like weight or completed workouts.
💰 PREMIUM FEATURES (UPSELL OPPORTUNITY)
Feature	Premium Upgrade Hook
Unlimited AI Chat Access	Deep questions, goal-setting convos, injury advice, motivation on demand.
Body-Part-Specific Workout Library	Full access to gym-style workouts, resistance training, HIIT, etc.
Video Workouts of YOU	Premium content = personal coaching. Feels more exclusive with your presence.
Blind Accessibility with Audio Workouts	Premium version has fully guided audio-only training with coaching.
Advanced Progress Tracker	See history, charts, personal bests, and upload before/after pics.
Exclusive Weekly Challenges with Rewards	Earn free merch, coaching calls, shoutouts, or supplement discounts.
VIP Community or Group Chat	Paid users join a private in-app or Telegram group led by you.
